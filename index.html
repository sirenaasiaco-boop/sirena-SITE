002 / About Us

Who
Are We

Sirena Asia is the region's premier mermaid performance collective — a collective of elite artists trained in underwater performance, breath-hold technique, and spectacle design.

Founded on the belief that the ocean holds the world's oldest magic, we bring that magic above the surface — into your events, activations, and celebrations.
