## Colors
- **Navy (Primary):** #001a4d — headers, backgrounds, deep sections
- **Cyan (Water):** #00bcd4 — accents, links, hover states, water motifs
- **Coral/Pink (Tail):** #ff6b9d — CTAs, highlights, performer accents
- **Deep Navy (Dark bg):** #000d26 — hero backgrounds, dark sections
- **Light Mist:** #e8f4f8 — light section backgrounds
- **White:** #ffffff — text on dark, card backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings:** Cinzel (Google Fonts) — elegant serif, classical, luxury feel
- **Subheadings:** Cormorant Garamond (Google Fonts) — refined, editorial
- **Body:** Inter or system-ui — clean, readable
- **Letter-spacing headings:** -0.03em on large display text
- **Line-height body:** 1.7

## Tone & Voice
Sirena Asia speaks with quiet confidence — luxurious but not cold, fantastical but professional. Copy should feel like stepping into a myth: evocative, immersive, and aspirational. Avoid corporate jargon. Avoid cutesy or childish language. Write for adults booking premium experiences.
